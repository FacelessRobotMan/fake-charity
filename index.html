Duds For Spuds

In today's world, there are charities for every group, cause or belief that you can think of. But has anyone ever thought about potatoes and how they never seem to have any clothes? Well, we here at Duds For Spuds did.

Spuds For Duds recognizes the need to provide needy potatoes with the proper clothing that they deserve but are often denied.

For a small nominal donation of your choosing you can make sure that one potato will get the articles of clothing that they need to become who they were truly meant to be.
